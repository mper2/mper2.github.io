The Spotify Map, 2020

Course project, Advanced Projectcourse in Interactive Media Technology.
The Spotify Map is a concept created as a way of storing and navigating precious music memories. It enables you to add songs to locations where they have special meaning. You can share the music memories with friends and you will be reminded of the memories when you visit the locations.
Collaborators: Axel Johansson, Elna Mattsson Johansson, Emma Johansson & Johan Marbinah

ALLY, 2020

Course project, User Experience Design and Evaluation.
ALLY is a platform prototype developed in Figma for a project on Futuring Peace. It is a platform for increasing women's involvement in the work against violence towards women in conflict.
Collaborators: Emma Johansson

Krona, 2020

Course project, Execution - Running Your Own Company.
Krona is subconscious micro investment company, created as a project in the course Execution at Stockholm School of Economics. A hi-fi prototype of the app was developed to give users an insight into the service provided by Krona.

Stockholm Sound Map, 2020

Course project, Sound in Interaction.
Stockholm Sound Map is an interactive map that with the help of a motion capture system gives a sonic deptiction of a future Stockholm where climate change has left its mark.
Collaborators: Emma Johansson

  • Listen to Stockholm

Tinderfy, 2019

Course project, Interation Programming and the Dynamic Web.
Tinderfy is just what is sounds like, a mix between Spotify and Tinder. You can make you own custom playlist by first choosing genre, then either swipe right if you like the suggested song or left if you don't. That playlist will then be added to your Spotify account.
Collaborators: Elna Mattsson Johansson, Emma Johansson, Fanny Curtsson
